Thor: The Norse God of Thunder

Marvel Universe Hero

Who is Thor?

Thor is an Asgardian god of thunder, son of Odin, and a founding member of the Avengers who wields the magical hammer Mjolnir (and sometimes Stormbreaker),
granting him control over storms and flight. Known for his divine strength, durability, and combat skills, he protects the Nine Realms and Earth, though his path to heroism is marked by
banishment to Earth and learning humility. He is also the God of Thunder, wielding his powers with the legendary hammer Mjolnir and other enchanted weapons.
