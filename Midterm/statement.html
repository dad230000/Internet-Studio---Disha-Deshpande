Design Statement ✦

Visual Language & Typography

The palette uses Aegean blues with white-plaster neutrals and warm terracotta accents to mirror Cycladic architecture and sun-baked pottery. Headings use Cinzel for a classic, Greek-inspired feel; body text uses Inter for clarity and accessibility.

Rounded cards and soft shadows echo whitewashed curves and domes. Large, edge-to-edge imagery honors Santorini’s photographic appeal.

Interaction & UI Choices

A sticky nav keeps page jumps close while comparing activities and dining. CTA buttons—Book Now, Request Info, Contact Us—repeat across pages so visitors always have a next step.

Accessibility: semantic HTML (proper headings/labels), descriptive alt text, and high-contrast colors for legibility on screens and in sunlight.
