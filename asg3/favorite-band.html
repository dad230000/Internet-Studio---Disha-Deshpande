Overview

KATSEYE blew up because they're a high-profile HYBE x Geffen project formed on the global audition series The Debut: Dream Academy,
then introduced to an even wider audience through Netflix's Pop Star Academy: KATSEYE; they followed that momentum with slick,
hooky releases—starting with the single “Debut” and their first EP SIS (Soft Is Strong)—and headline-grabbing stages like their 2025
MTV VMAs debut and charting singles, which cemented them as a rising pop act with international members and big-label backing.
