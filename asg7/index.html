About the Candidate

Short bio: Sir Spaghetti was born in a humble kitchen pot in Italy. Raised among marinara and parmesan, he quickly learned the art of flexibility—literally. He's long, he's strong, and he's al dente when it comes to leadership.

Why he would make a good ruler: Sir Spaghetti believes in unity—he never breaks under pressure and always brings people together, usually around a dinner table. He's a symbol of comfort, warmth, and delicious teamwork.
